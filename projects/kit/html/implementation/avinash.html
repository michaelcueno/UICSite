Implementation Phase

On the right is the break down of what everyone contributed to the project.

My part of the project is the Cabinet Pane. First let's look at the cabinet pane. This pane contains the inventory and the grocery list. Here is a screenshot of the pane

Lets look at the pane in detail. It has a design of a wooden and steel cabinet. As you first come to the page, the sliding door opens to the right automatically.

Once the door is opened, we can see all the items in two different sets one is perishables and the other one is non perishables. Both the sets are marked for identification.

The perishables displays the items name, quantity, date of manfacutreing and date of expiry. Once you select an item by clicking on it, the app displays the items details a bit bottom of the list. This option lets you edit the details of the item, change anything you entered wrong, increase or decrease the quantity of
